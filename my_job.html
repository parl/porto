The Problem

Muhammadiyah University Yogyakarta (UMY) has a considerable number of employees involved in managing its campus. UMY plans to develop an application that can provide facilities for managing information related to tasks, including assignments, deadlines, and job statuses.

This aids in monitoring project progress and ensuring that tasks are completed according to schedule.

Design & Develop Process

I am responsible for designing the UI/UX of the My Job application and contribute to the development of the application using the Flutter framework.

The design process involves creating sketches, wireframes, and prototypes, followed by slicing using Flutter for implementation.
