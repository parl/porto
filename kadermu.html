Kadermu is a comprehensive information system application designed specifically for the cadre development program of Muhammadiyah. This application streamlines the entire process of managing cadre activities within the organization. It includes features for creating and organizing cadre events, conducting pretests and posttests for each event to assess participant knowledge and progress, managing event attendance, and issuing certificates to participants upon completion. The application aims to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of the cadre development process, ensuring that all activities are well-documented and participants' achievements are properly recognized.

In developing Kadermu, I served as the full-stack Laravel developer responsible for both the front-end and back-end components of the application. Leveraging the Laravel framework, I created a robust and user-friendly interface that allows administrators to easily manage events and track participant progress. The application incorporates a variety of modules that are seamlessly integrated to provide a cohesive experience for users. These modules include event management, assessment tools, attendance tracking, and certificate generation, each designed to meet the specific needs of the Muhammadiyah cadre development program.

The database stack for Kadermu is powered by PostgreSQL, chosen for its reliability and performance in handling large volumes of data. PostgreSQL's advanced features such as support for complex queries, robust data integrity, and scalability made it an ideal choice for this application. Throughout the development process, I focused on creating a scalable and maintainable codebase, ensuring that Kadermu can grow and evolve alongside the needs of Muhammadiyah. This project showcases my ability to develop comprehensive information systems that address specific organizational requirements while providing a seamless user experience.

Since its deployed in April 2024, Kadermu has quickly gained traction, with over 1,000 users actively utilizing the platform for cadre development activities. Accessible via kader.muhammadiyah.or.id, Kadermu has successfully supported five cadre events, demonstrating its effectiveness and reliability. The positive reception and growing user base highlight the application's robust design and user-friendly interface, ensuring that it meets the needs of the Muhammadiyah community efficiently.
