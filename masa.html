MASA app is a super app owned by Muhammadiyah, integrating essential features such as prayer schedules, Quranic resources, Qibla direction finder, Bukumu (bookstore), Eventmu (events), and Chat HPT (Himpunan Putusan Tarjih), an AI trained according to Muhammadiyah's religious rulings. Within the MASA app, my role involved creating APIs for the Quranic and Bukumu functionalities. As a full-stack Laravel developer, I utilized MongoDB as the backend database stack for efficient data management and scalability.

My responsibilities included designing and developing robust APIs that power the Quranic and Bukumu modules within the MASA app. Leveraging Laravel's framework, I focused on ensuring seamless integration and functionality across different app features, enhancing user experience and accessibility to religious and educational resources. MongoDB's flexibility and performance capabilities were instrumental in handling diverse data requirements and optimizing data retrieval processes within the app ecosystem.

The MASA app project underscores my proficiency in full-stack web development, specifically in Laravel and MongoDB environments. By contributing to the creation of essential APIs for Quranic and book-related functionalities, I aimed to support Muhammadiyah's mission of providing accessible and reliable religious information and educational resources through modern digital platforms.
