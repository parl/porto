Wordpress Developer

At BSI UAD, the Information Systems Bureau of Ahmad Dahlan University, I undertook the role of a WordPress developer. Specifically, I developed and rebuilt the university's profile website using the Enfold theme. My responsibilities included redesigning the entire website based on provided designs, handling layouting, content population, and optimizing the site for SEO and performance. This involved ensuring that the website not only met design specifications but also achieved high standards in search engine optimization and user experience, enhancing both visibility and functionality for its online audience.

This project demonstrated my proficiency in WordPress development, theme customization using Enfold, and my ability to deliver websites that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also optimized for performance and search engines. Through meticulous attention to detail and adherence to best practices in web development, I contributed to enhancing the digital presence of BSI UAD, aligning the website with modern standards in web design and functionality.
