Bukumu is an application designed for purchasing and reading books, whether in physical or ebook formats. This comprehensive app handles all aspects of book purchasing and reading systems. It facilitates publishers in publishing books, setting prices, and enables users to seamlessly buy and read books. Users can purchase, read, and provide ratings for books through the MASA app available on the Play Store. As a full-stack Laravel developer, I played a crucial role in developing the book API tailored for integration with the mobile app.

In developing Bukumu, I utilized the Laravel framework and MongoDB database stack to ensure robust performance and scalability. The application's backend development involved creating secure APIs that enable smooth interactions between users and publishers, facilitating seamless book purchasing, reading, and rating experiences. My responsibilities also included optimizing database queries and ensuring data integrity, contributing to a reliable and efficient application ecosystem.

The Bukumu project showcases my expertise in full-stack web development, particularly in Laravel and MongoDB environments, emphasizing my ability to deliver functional and user-friendly solutions for complex applications like digital bookstores. By leveraging modern technologies and best practices, I aimed to enhance user satisfaction and operational efficiency within the digital book purchasing and reading experience.

Since its launch in December 2023, Bukumu has gained popularity among over 1000 users. The platform has integrated seamlessly with the MASA app, available on the Play Store, where users can access and read a growing collection of 9 books. Bukumu facilitates easy purchasing, reading, and rating of books, offering a user-friendly experience for both publishers and readers alike. Additionally, Bukumu can be accessed through buku.muhammadiyah.or.id, further expanding its reach and accessibility. As the full-stack Laravel developer behind Bukumu, I ensured the application's robustness and scalability, including the development of an API for integration with the mobile app. This project underscores my ability to deliver comprehensive digital solutions that enhance accessibility and enjoyment of literature across multiple platforms.
