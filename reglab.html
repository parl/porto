RegLab is a practicum information system developed specifically to support laboratory activities in the Computer Science Department at UAD. Our focus in this project was on creating a solution that simplifies the management and execution of practicums while enhancing the student experience during their lab sessions.

RegLab provides a comprehensive system for tracking student attendance during practicum sessions. Supervisors can easily monitor and record attendance, fostering an organized and efficient learning environment. In this system, my role involved developing the attendance system for lab assistants and faculty members, ensuring that instructors can effectively monitor lab assistant attendance. The technology stack utilized Laravel with MySQL as the backend database, enabling robust performance and data management capabilities.

Since its launch in May 2023, RegLab has efficiently managed over 1,000 students per semester in handling registration and attendance for practicums. The application has significantly streamlined the process for students, lab assistants, and faculty members at the Informatics Department, UAD. Accessible via reglab.tif.uad.ac.id, RegLab has proven indispensable in enhancing the practicum management workflow, ensuring a more organized and efficient experience for all stakeholders involved.
